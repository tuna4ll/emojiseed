🌱 emojiseed

BIP39 style mnemonics, but with emojis — 4096 emojis, 12 bits each.

Randomness comes from your browser's crypto.getRandomValues. Nothing leaves this page.

Runs the exact same C core as the CLI, compiled to WebAssembly. Everything is computed locally in your browser.
